I am also purusing **B.S. in Data Science and Applications** from **[IIT Madras](https://onlinedegree.iitm.ac.in/)** and recently finsihed my **Diploma in Data Science** & **Diploma in Programming**.

I do a lot of different things in different domains. I try to maintain a list of major projects/works I am/was invovled in:

- I was a [GSoC 2025 contributor](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/programs/2025/projects/5GJMolBG) developing Solar Generation Forecasting models on publicly available data. I still continue to work on this, It is an open project and if you want to contribute , here is the [link](https://github.com/openclimatefix/open-data-pvnet). I would be happy to help anyway I can.

- I was also an [ESoC 2025 contributor](https://www.esoc.dev/) working on [**AI Drug development and Aptamer designing**](https://github.com/european-summer-of-code/esoc2025/blob/main/cards/aptamer.md), As of January,2026 work is going [here](https://github.com/gc-os-ai/pyaptamer), but we hope to release a v1.0.0 soon.
